About Us

Hiep Thanh Timber specializes in manufacturing and distributing robber-wood finger-joint panels. With a high standard in manufacturing process, we constantly strive to improve the quality of our product in order to meet customers high expectation

Although Hiep Thanh Timber is a new player in the industry, our finger-joint panels have been proven to be one of the most reliable product in the market. Hiep Thanh Timbers products have been trusted and used by many companies and organizations.
